Class: Cooked Vegetable

Attributes:
vegetableSkin (Boolean)
vegetableType (String)
saltPercentage (Integer)
addedButter% (Integer)

Methods:
removeVegetableSkin (modifies vegetableSkin attribute to "No")
changeVegetableType (changes vegetableType attribute)
NoSalt (changes saltPercentage attribute to 0%)
Flavorize (increases addedButter% attribute by 20%)
